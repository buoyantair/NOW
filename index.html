ThoughtWorks N.O.W. is a growing and connected community of women from all walks of life, looking to be a part of an open and inclusive network, to come together to learn from each other and be inspired. It provides a positive and motivating environment by bringing fresh content and wonderful speakers.

After successfully conducting the events in Bangalore and Pune, for the first time, we are launching the event in Hyderabad. It is a one-day event to celebrate women leaders. Our goal is to facilitate an aspirational, relevant and engaging dialogue with leaders across domains and translate these experiences

We have an exciting day planned with keynote, sessions, panel discussion and a workshop. There is a stellar line up of speakers, each with a unique and inspiring story to share.

Speakers
Nayana Udipi

Nayana Udipi is currently part of ThoughtWorks' India marketing team. Prior to ThoughtWorks, Nayana worked with Sangama, an NGO that works to secure the rights of sexual minorities. Nayana's progressive thought is a regular feature at LGBTQI related conferences, workshops and panel discussions.

Rema Rajeshwari

The Telangana Superintendent of Police wields her baton and pen with equal ease. Through her collaborative policing efforts, she encourages women to break gender stereotypes and empower them to emerge as leaders. She writes widely on policing, women, children, and the less fortunate with a passion for scholarly accuracy.

Dr. Vijaya Krishnan

Dr. Vijaya Krishnan is a Certified Professional Midwife (CPM), and the co-founder and Director of The Sanctum, a Natural Birth Centre. She believes strongly that respectful maternity care is the most important key in helping women to have an informed and empowered child birth.

Anupama Dalmia

Anupama Dalmia is a multiple award winning blogger, author, influencer, creative writing mentor and a multi-faceted entrepreneur. Her journey of having resigned from a lucrative IT job to pursue her passions and build a career from scratch has been covered extensively to motivate people to believe in themselves and never say “never”.

Ranjana Naik

Ranjana Naik is the Founder Director of Swan Suites serviced apartments in Hyderabad. An ISB alumnus and a scholar from Goldman Sachs, she has 17 years of experience as an entrepreneur in Banking service, Telecom and Hospitality.

Piya Bahadur

Piya Bahadur completed a 17,000-kilometre all women motorcycle expedition last year from Hyderabad to Vietnam and back. Previously, Piya was the Regional Director of the United States-India Education Foundation (USIEF), has also worked on Entrepreneurship Development at The Indian School of Business (ISB),

Dr. Shanta Thoutam

Shanta is the Vice President of Corporate Innovation & Business Development at T-hub. As a Woman Innovation Leader in the largest Incubator of India, she has been supporting not only the overall entrepreneurial ecosystem to thrive, but also the Women Entrepreneurs, Women in Tech, and Girls in Tech on how to carve a niche for themselves.
